description=Living Books is a framework that brings fully readable books to Project Zomboid.
description=
description=This first release includes [b]Don Quixote[/b], with more books, features, and improvements planned for future updates.
description=
description=[h2]✨ Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Automatic chapter-based pagination.
description=[*]Automatic page detection with built-in search.
description=[*]Automatically saves your reading progress.
description=[*]Custom keybindings for page navigation and quick exit.
description=[*]Highly optimized. A 400-page book can take as little as 1 MB.
description=[*]Books are loaded during game startup for smooth performance.
description=[*]Sandbox option to configure book spawn rate.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]⚠ Important Notes[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Living Books is currently in an experimental phase (Initial Release).
description=[*]Backing up your save before installing is recommended.
description=[*]Multiplayer has not been fully tested, but it should work without issues.
description=[*]The mod can generally be added to or removed from an existing save safely.
description=[*]To obtain the book after installing the mod, item respawn must be enabled or you will need to visit unexplored areas.
description=[/list]
